Spicy Orange Chicken Nuggets

Crockpot

Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 3 hr 30 min Total Time: 3 hr 45 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 8 Source: Dump Meals by Cathy Mitchell

Ingredients

1 bag (28oz) frozen popcorn chicken bites

1 1/2 cups prepared honey teriyaki marinade

3/4 cup orange juice concentrate

2/3 cup water

1 tbsp orange marmalade

1/2 tsp hot chile sauce OR Sriracha

orange peel (optional)

Hot cooked rice with peas and corn

Directions

Combine chicken, teriyaki marinade orange juice concentrate, water, marmalade, and chile sauce into Crockpot; stir to blend. Cover; cook on low 3-3 1/2 hours.

Sprinkle with orange peel and serve with rice.
